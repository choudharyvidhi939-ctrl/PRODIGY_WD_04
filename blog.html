Welcome to My Blog

Hi, I’m Vidhi Choudhary! This blog is a little corner where I share my journey of learning, experimenting, and growing as a developer. My path into the world of coding has been full of curiosity, challenges, and exciting discoveries.

I first started learning the basics of computers and programming during my college years. Classes introduced me to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I still remember the excitement of seeing my first “Hello World” website come alive in a browser. Those small wins motivated me to dive deeper into the field of web development.

Outside of college, I enrolled in computer classes where I learned about PHP, databases, and more structured programming concepts. These classes gave me confidence and helped me understand how different technologies come together to create functional applications.

Alongside my formal learning, I also started exploring online resources. YouTube tutorials became my late-night teachers — guiding me through concepts like React, CSS Grid, Flexbox, and Python scripting. I also practiced small projects at home, like creating simple apps and experimenting with new designs, which really strengthened my confidence.

One of the most exciting projects I worked on was a Movie Playlist Manager in Python. It was challenging at first, but it helped me connect theory with practical problem-solving. I also created mini React apps to practice component-based development and improve my design sense.

Looking back, I feel proud of how far I’ve come. From learning basics in college, sharpening skills in computer classes, and pushing myself with self-learning on YouTube — each step has shaped me into who I am today. And this is just the beginning! I look forward to building bigger projects, writing tutorials, and sharing more of my experiences here in this blog.
